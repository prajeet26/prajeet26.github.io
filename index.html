Research updates

MedProm: Bridging Dialogue Gaps in Healthcare with Knowledge-Enhanced Generative Models
ACM Transactions on Computing for Healthcare
PAPER

MedProm is a knowledge-enhanced generative model for medical dialogue systems, integrating the MediConnect Graph to improve context-aware responses. It leverages BioGPT and curriculum learning for precise, coherent, and medically relevant conversations.

D Varshney, N Behera, P Katari, A Ekbal

Multimodal BERT for Identifying Claims in Tweets
CLEF 2022, Bologna, Italy
PAPER

A Multimodal BERT-based approach for identifying claim check-worthiness, verifiability, and harmful content in tweets, achieving top-3 ranks in CheckThat! Lab 2022.

M Suri, P Katari, S Dudeja
